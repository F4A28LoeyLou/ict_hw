  1. Health Promoting Team

    2. The Health Promoting Team is a team which is leaded by our school health care co-ordinator and is formed by a group of students who are enthusiastic in promoting health. In this team, students can learn the basic knowledge of first aid and how to live a healthy lifestyle. Moreover, they can put what they have learned into practice by doing some duty work in the clinc, or on some sepcial ocassion events (e.g. sports day).

  2. German Class

    3. This course is designed for beginners who are interested in learning German. It is mainly based on practical and natural situations, supplemented by grammar. It also introduces German culture, so that students can expand their international vision while learning the language. Student will learn from the alphabet and pronunciation rules first, follow by some basic sentence patterns, life conversations and tense changes. In addition to lecture notes, multimedia teaching materials are also supplemented. It is expected that by the end of the course, students will be able to conduct daily conversations in German, such as describing events, ordering meals, shopping, etc.

  3. The English Club

    4. The English Club aims to promote in its Club and in the school campus the use of English in a relaxed and enjoyable way. It strives to arouse and promote students’ interest and usage in English by organizing diverse English activities and games in which students are guided to utilize structures and language in different circumstances.
    The Club's motto:“Let’s set our heart ablaze with a passion for English.”
